## Why November?

November is shoulder season in most of the world — ideal for travellers seeking good weather, fewer crowds, and better prices.

## Top Destinations

### Rajasthan, India

High: **29°C (84°F)** | Low: **13°C (55°F)**

Post-monsoon clarity — blue skies, cooler temps, and vibrant Pushkar and Diwali festivals.

### Marrakech, Morocco

High: **21°C (70°F)** | Low: **9°C (48°F)**

Mild and sunny — perfect for exploring the medina, souks, and Atlas foothills without summer heat.

### Vietnam, Southeast Asia

High: **27°C (81°F)** | Low: **18°C (64°F)**

Central and southern Vietnam are at their best — dry, warm, and less crowded than December.

### Canary Islands, Spain

High: **24°C (75°F)** | Low: **17°C (63°F)**

Europe's warmest November — beach days, volcanic hikes, and affordable flights.

### New Zealand, Oceania

High: **18°C (64°F)** | Low: **10°C (50°F)**

Spring is in full swing — lambing season, wildflowers, and hiking with long daylight.

## How to Choose

| Priority | Best Fit |
|----------|----------|
| Beach and sun | Rajasthan or Marrakech |
| Culture and sightseeing | Vietnam or Canary Islands |
| Budget-friendly | Research shoulder-season pricing for each destination |
| Adventure | New Zealand |

## Packing Tips for November Travel

Shoulder season months mean variable weather. Pack layers and a rain jacket regardless of destination.
