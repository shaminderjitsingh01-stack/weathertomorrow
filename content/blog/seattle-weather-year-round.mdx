## Overview

Seattle, USA — Seattle's reputation for rain is slightly exaggerated — it drizzles often but total rainfall is less than New York or Miami; summers are gloriously dry. Whether you are planning a holiday or a business trip, understanding the weather helps you pack right and make the most of your time.

## Temperature

Daytime highs reach **16°C (61°F)** while nights cool to **8°C (46°F)**. Expect around 5 hours of sunshine per day.

## Rainfall

Seattle sees about **95mm of rainfall across 12 days** during this period. Showers are fairly regular — keep an umbrella handy when you head out.

## Humidity and Comfort

Average humidity sits at **75%**. Most visitors find this comfortable, though midday can feel slightly muggy.

## What to Pack

- Layered outfits — sweaters, a warm jacket, and a scarf
- Waterproof walking shoes
- Compact umbrella or light rain jacket
- Comfortable walking shoes suited to the terrain

## Best Things to Do

- Explore Pike Place Market and watch the fishmongers toss salmon
- Head to the Space Needle and Chihuly Garden for glass-art wonder
- Take a ferry to Bainbridge Island for a scenic day trip
- Tour the coffee roasters that made Seattle the espresso capital of America

## Travel Tips

- Locals rarely use umbrellas — a good waterproof shell is the way to go
- July and August are almost always sunny and dry; the best time to visit
- Puget Sound keeps temperatures moderate; it rarely gets very hot or very cold
