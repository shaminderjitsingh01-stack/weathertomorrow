## Why December?

December offers two choices: embrace winter with Christmas markets and snow, or escape it with tropical beaches and southern summer sunshine.

## Top Destinations

### Vienna, Austria

High: **3°C (37°F)** | Low: **-2°C (28°F)**

The quintessential Christmas market experience — Glühwein, gingerbread, and imperial charm.

### Maldives, Indian Ocean

High: **30°C (86°F)** | Low: **26°C (79°F)**

Dry northeast monsoon brings calm seas and pristine visibility. Peak luxury escape.

### Lapland, Finland

High: **-8°C (18°F)** | Low: **-15°C (5°F)**

Snow-guaranteed Christmas: Northern Lights, husky sleds, and meeting Santa.

### Cape Town, South Africa

High: **27°C (81°F)** | Low: **16°C (61°F)**

Southern summer — Table Mountain, wine country, and beaches in warm sunshine.

### Cartagena, Colombia

High: **32°C (90°F)** | Low: **25°C (77°F)**

Dry season begins. Colonial old town, Caribbean coast, and festive pre-Christmas energy.

## How to Choose

| Priority | Best Fit |
|----------|----------|
| Beach and sun | Vienna or Maldives |
| Culture and sightseeing | Lapland or Cape Town |
| Budget-friendly | Research shoulder-season pricing for each destination |
| Adventure | Cartagena |

## Packing Tips for December Travel

Northern hemisphere destinations are in winter — pack warm layers. Southern hemisphere destinations are in summer — go light.
