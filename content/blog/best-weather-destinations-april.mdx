## Why April?

April brings spring blooms to Europe and the Mediterranean. Crowds are lighter than summer, and temperatures are ideal for walking, cycling, and sightseeing.

## Top Destinations

### Amsterdam, Netherlands

High: **14°C (57°F)** | Low: **6°C (43°F)**

Tulip season at Keukenhof, canal cruises, and the city emerging from winter on two wheels.

### Lisbon, Portugal

High: **19°C (66°F)** | Low: **12°C (54°F)**

Warm sunshine, blooming jacarandas, and the city at its most photogenic before summer crowds.

### Istanbul, Turkey

High: **16°C (61°F)** | Low: **9°C (48°F)**

The Istanbul Tulip Festival fills parks with millions of blooms. Mild weather for mosque and bazaar visits.

### Jordan, Middle East

High: **25°C (77°F)** | Low: **12°C (54°F)**

Ideal for Petra and Wadi Rum — warm days, cool nights, and wildflowers in the desert.

### Bali, Indonesia

High: **31°C (88°F)** | Low: **24°C (75°F)**

Tail end of wet season means lush green rice terraces, fewer crowds, and lower prices.

## How to Choose

| Priority | Best Fit |
|----------|----------|
| Beach and sun | Amsterdam or Lisbon |
| Culture and sightseeing | Istanbul or Jordan |
| Budget-friendly | Research shoulder-season pricing for each destination |
| Adventure | Bali |

## Packing Tips for April Travel

Shoulder season months mean variable weather. Pack layers and a rain jacket regardless of destination.
