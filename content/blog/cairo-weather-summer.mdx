## Overview

Cairo, Egypt — summer brings extreme desert heat, but also thinner crowds at the Pyramids and lower hotel prices. Whether you are planning a holiday or a business trip, understanding the weather helps you pack right and make the most of your time.

## Temperature

Daytime highs reach **36°C (97°F)** while nights cool to **22°C (72°F)**. Expect around 12 hours of sunshine per day.

## Rainfall

Cairo sees about **0mm of rainfall across 0 days** during this period. Rain is virtually nonexistent — expect clear, dry skies throughout.

## Humidity and Comfort

Average humidity sits at **35%**. The air is pleasantly dry, making even warmer temperatures feel manageable.

## What to Pack

- Lightweight breathable clothing in cotton or linen
- High-SPF sunscreen, sunglasses, and a wide-brimmed hat
- Comfortable walking shoes suited to the terrain

## Best Things to Do

- Visit the Pyramids of Giza at sunrise before temperatures soar
- Take a felucca sailboat ride on the Nile at sunset
- Explore the Egyptian Museum and its air-conditioned galleries
- Haggle in Khan el-Khalili bazaar during the cooler evening hours

## Travel Tips

- Start your day at dawn — by 10 am the heat becomes punishing
- Drink at least 3 litres of water daily to avoid dehydration
- Dress modestly in loose, light-coloured fabrics that also respect local customs
