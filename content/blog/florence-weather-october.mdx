## Overview

Florence, Italy — October offers golden autumn light, harvest festivals, and far fewer tourists than the summer crush. Whether you are planning a holiday or a business trip, understanding the weather helps you pack right and make the most of your time.

## Temperature

Daytime highs reach **19°C (66°F)** while nights cool to **10°C (50°F)**. Expect around 5 hours of sunshine per day.

## Rainfall

Florence sees about **90mm of rainfall across 8 days** during this period. Showers are fairly regular — keep an umbrella handy when you head out.

## Humidity and Comfort

Average humidity sits at **70%**. Most visitors find this comfortable, though midday can feel slightly muggy.

## What to Pack

- Layered outfits — sweaters, a warm jacket, and a scarf
- Waterproof walking shoes
- Compact umbrella or light rain jacket
- Comfortable walking shoes suited to the terrain

## Best Things to Do

- Spend a morning in the Uffizi Gallery without summer queues
- Climb the Duomo dome for panoramic views of autumn-tinted Tuscany
- Drive through the Chianti countryside for wine harvest celebrations
- Enjoy a truffle-season menu at a traditional Tuscan trattoria

## Travel Tips

- Rain showers are common — a waterproof jacket is more practical than an umbrella in narrow streets
- Book the Uffizi and Accademia online even in shoulder season
- The evening chill arrives quickly after sunset; bring a warm layer for dinners al fresco
