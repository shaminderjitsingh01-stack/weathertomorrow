## Overview

Bali, Indonesia — the dry season from April to October is Bali's peak time — expect sunny days, calm seas for snorkelling, and spectacular sunsets. Whether you are planning a holiday or a business trip, understanding the weather helps you pack right and make the most of your time.

## Temperature

Daytime highs reach **31°C (88°F)** while nights cool to **23°C (73°F)**. Expect around 8 hours of sunshine per day.

## Rainfall

Bali sees about **55mm of rainfall across 5 days** during this period. Rainfall is moderate. The odd shower is possible but unlikely to ruin your plans.

## Humidity and Comfort

Average humidity sits at **70%**. Most visitors find this comfortable, though midday can feel slightly muggy.

## What to Pack

- Lightweight breathable clothing in cotton or linen
- High-SPF sunscreen, sunglasses, and a wide-brimmed hat
- Compact umbrella or light rain jacket
- Comfortable walking shoes suited to the terrain

## Best Things to Do

- Watch sunrise from the rim of Mount Batur volcano
- Walk through the emerald Tegallalang rice terraces near Ubud
- Surf the breaks at Uluwatu or Canggu
- Visit Tanah Lot sea temple at sunset

## Travel Tips

- Dry season is peak tourist season — book accommodation and activities ahead
- Rent a scooter for flexibility, but get an international driving permit first
- Respect temple dress codes: sarong and sash are required at most Balinese temples
