## Overview

Los Angeles, USA — LA's Mediterranean climate delivers sunshine roughly 280 days a year, making it one of the most consistently sunny major cities in the world. Whether you are planning a holiday or a business trip, understanding the weather helps you pack right and make the most of your time.

## Temperature

Daytime highs reach **25°C (77°F)** while nights cool to **14°C (57°F)**. Expect around 10 hours of sunshine per day.

## Rainfall

Los Angeles sees about **35mm of rainfall across 3 days** during this period. Rainfall is moderate. The odd shower is possible but unlikely to ruin your plans.

## Humidity and Comfort

Average humidity sits at **60%**. Most visitors find this comfortable, though midday can feel slightly muggy.

## What to Pack

- Light layers — t-shirts plus a jacket for cooler evenings
- Sunscreen and sunglasses
- Compact umbrella or light rain jacket
- Comfortable walking shoes suited to the terrain

## Best Things to Do

- Hike to the Griffith Observatory for Hollywood sign and city views
- Catch the sunset at Santa Monica Pier or Venice Beach boardwalk
- Drive Pacific Coast Highway through Malibu to Zuma Beach
- Explore the Getty Center for art, architecture, and hilltop panoramas

## Travel Tips

- Traffic is legendary — use the Metro for downtown, Hollywood, and Santa Monica
- June Gloom brings morning marine fog that burns off by noon; do not be discouraged
- Neighbourhoods vary wildly in temperature — the Valley is often 5-8 degrees hotter than the coast
