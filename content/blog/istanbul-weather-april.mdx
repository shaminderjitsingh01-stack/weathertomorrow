## Overview

Istanbul, Turkey — April brings the famous Istanbul Tulip Festival, mild temperatures, and fewer crowds than summer. Whether you are planning a holiday or a business trip, understanding the weather helps you pack right and make the most of your time.

## Temperature

Daytime highs reach **16°C (61°F)** while nights cool to **9°C (48°F)**. Expect around 7 hours of sunshine per day.

## Rainfall

Istanbul sees about **45mm of rainfall across 9 days** during this period. Rainfall is moderate. The odd shower is possible but unlikely to ruin your plans.

## Humidity and Comfort

Average humidity sits at **70%**. Most visitors find this comfortable, though midday can feel slightly muggy.

## What to Pack

- Layered outfits — sweaters, a warm jacket, and a scarf
- Waterproof walking shoes
- Compact umbrella or light rain jacket
- Comfortable walking shoes suited to the terrain

## Best Things to Do

- Marvel at the Hagia Sophia and Blue Mosque without peak-season queues
- Stroll through Emirgan Park during the Tulip Festival with millions of blooms
- Cruise the Bosphorus at sunset for views of two continents
- Bargain for spices and ceramics in the Grand Bazaar

## Travel Tips

- Weather can be changeable — dress in layers and carry a rain jacket
- Eat like a local: try simit for breakfast and balik ekmek at the Galata Bridge
- The Istanbul Museum Pass saves money and queue time at major sites
