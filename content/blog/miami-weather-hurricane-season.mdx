## Overview

Miami, USA — hurricane season from June to November brings the cheapest hotel rates, dramatic skies, and warm ocean water — just keep an eye on the forecasts. Whether you are planning a holiday or a business trip, understanding the weather helps you pack right and make the most of your time.

## Temperature

Daytime highs reach **33°C (91°F)** while nights cool to **25°C (77°F)**. Expect around 8 hours of sunshine per day.

## Rainfall

Miami sees about **200mm of rainfall across 15 days** during this period. Downpours can be heavy but usually pass quickly. Waterproof gear is essential.

## Humidity and Comfort

Average humidity sits at **75%**. Most visitors find this comfortable, though midday can feel slightly muggy.

## What to Pack

- Lightweight breathable clothing in cotton or linen
- High-SPF sunscreen, sunglasses, and a wide-brimmed hat
- Waterproof jacket and a sturdy umbrella
- Comfortable walking shoes suited to the terrain

## Best Things to Do

- Soak up the sun and art-deco architecture on South Beach
- Explore the Wynwood Walls street art district
- Take an airboat tour through the Everglades
- Enjoy Cuban coffee and croquetas in Little Havana

## Travel Tips

- Monitor the National Hurricane Center during your trip and have a plan
- Afternoon thunderstorms are almost daily — they pass quickly
- Travel insurance with hurricane coverage is strongly recommended
