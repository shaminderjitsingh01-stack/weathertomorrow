## Why July?

July is peak summer — the longest days, warmest waters, and biggest festivals. Book early and embrace the season.

## Top Destinations

### Stockholm, Sweden

High: **22°C (72°F)** | Low: **14°C (57°F)**

Nearly 19 hours of daylight, archipelago islands, and the city at its outdoor best.

### Lake Bled, Slovenia

High: **26°C (79°F)** | Low: **14°C (57°F)**

Warm enough for lake swimming, hiking in the Julian Alps, and castle views.

### Canadian Rockies, Canada

High: **22°C (72°F)** | Low: **7°C (45°F)**

Banff and Jasper at their finest — hiking trails clear of snow, turquoise lakes, and wildlife.

### Bora Bora, French Polynesia

High: **29°C (84°F)** | Low: **24°C (75°F)**

Dry season in the South Pacific — overwater bungalows, clear lagoons, and tropical warmth.

### Edinburgh, UK

High: **19°C (66°F)** | Low: **11°C (52°F)**

Pre-Fringe warmth, long evenings, and the Royal Edinburgh Military Tattoo in late July.

## How to Choose

| Priority | Best Fit |
|----------|----------|
| Beach and sun | Stockholm or Lake Bled |
| Culture and sightseeing | Canadian Rockies or Bora Bora |
| Budget-friendly | Research shoulder-season pricing for each destination |
| Adventure | Edinburgh |

## Packing Tips for July Travel

Northern hemisphere destinations are in summer — pack light, breathable clothing and sunscreen. Southern hemisphere destinations are in winter — bring layers.
