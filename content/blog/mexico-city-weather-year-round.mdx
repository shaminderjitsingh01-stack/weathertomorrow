## Overview

Mexico City, Mexico — the high altitude keeps temperatures mild year-round despite being in the tropics, making it a surprisingly comfortable megacity. Whether you are planning a holiday or a business trip, understanding the weather helps you pack right and make the most of your time.

## Temperature

Daytime highs reach **25°C (77°F)** while nights cool to **12°C (54°F)**. Expect around 7 hours of sunshine per day.

## Rainfall

Mexico City sees about **135mm of rainfall across 14 days** during this period. Showers are fairly regular — keep an umbrella handy when you head out.

## Humidity and Comfort

Average humidity sits at **50%**. The air is pleasantly dry, making even warmer temperatures feel manageable.

## What to Pack

- Light layers — t-shirts plus a jacket for cooler evenings
- Sunscreen and sunglasses
- Waterproof jacket and a sturdy umbrella
- Comfortable walking shoes suited to the terrain

## Best Things to Do

- Visit the vast Zócalo and the Templo Mayor ruins
- Explore Frida Kahlo's Casa Azul in Coyoacán
- Wander Chapultepec Park and its world-class anthropology museum
- Eat tacos al pastor at a street stand in Condesa or Roma

## Travel Tips

- The rainy season runs May to October with afternoon showers — mornings stay dry
- Altitude sickness is rare at 2,200m but drink extra water your first day
- Uber and DiDi are safe and affordable for getting around
