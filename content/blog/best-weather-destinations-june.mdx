## Why June?

June kicks off summer in the Northern Hemisphere with long days, warm weather, and festivals. The Mediterranean enters peak season.

## Top Destinations

### Dubrovnik, Croatia

High: **27°C (81°F)** | Low: **19°C (66°F)**

Warm Adriatic waters, Game of Thrones sights, and the old town walls in golden light.

### Reykjavik, Iceland

High: **13°C (55°F)** | Low: **8°C (46°F)**

Midnight sun — 24 hours of daylight for the Golden Circle, whale watching, and highland roads.

### Provence, France

High: **28°C (82°F)** | Low: **16°C (61°F)**

Lavender fields begin blooming in late June. Markets, wine, and warm Provençal light.

### Tanzania, East Africa

High: **27°C (81°F)** | Low: **15°C (59°F)**

Dry season — the Great Migration herds are moving north through the Serengeti.

### Alaska, USA

High: **18°C (64°F)** | Low: **8°C (46°F)**

Long daylight, glaciers, wildlife, and the start of cruise season in mild temperatures.

## How to Choose

| Priority | Best Fit |
|----------|----------|
| Beach and sun | Dubrovnik or Reykjavik |
| Culture and sightseeing | Provence or Tanzania |
| Budget-friendly | Research shoulder-season pricing for each destination |
| Adventure | Alaska |

## Packing Tips for June Travel

Northern hemisphere destinations are in summer — pack light, breathable clothing and sunscreen. Southern hemisphere destinations are in winter — bring layers.
