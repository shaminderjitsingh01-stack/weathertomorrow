## Overview

Bangkok, Thailand — April is the hottest month of the year and home to the legendary Songkran water festival. Whether you are planning a holiday or a business trip, understanding the weather helps you pack right and make the most of your time.

## Temperature

Daytime highs reach **35°C (95°F)** while nights cool to **26°C (79°F)**. Expect around 8 hours of sunshine per day.

## Rainfall

Bangkok sees about **85mm of rainfall across 8 days** during this period. Showers are fairly regular — keep an umbrella handy when you head out.

## Humidity and Comfort

Average humidity sits at **80%**. Combined with the heat this can feel oppressive. Stay hydrated, wear breathable fabrics, and take advantage of air-conditioned spaces during peak afternoon hours.

## What to Pack

- Lightweight breathable clothing in cotton or linen
- High-SPF sunscreen, sunglasses, and a wide-brimmed hat
- Compact umbrella or light rain jacket
- Moisture-wicking fabrics and a refillable water bottle
- Comfortable walking shoes suited to the terrain

## Best Things to Do

- Explore the Grand Palace and Wat Pho before the midday heat
- Join Songkran water-fight celebrations on Khao San Road
- Cruise the Chao Phraya River on a long-tail boat at sunset
- Browse Chatuchak Weekend Market in the cool morning hours

## Travel Tips

- Avoid outdoor sightseeing between noon and 3 pm when heat peaks
- Carry a towel and waterproof phone case during Songkran week
- Air-conditioned malls like Siam Paragon make great afternoon retreats
