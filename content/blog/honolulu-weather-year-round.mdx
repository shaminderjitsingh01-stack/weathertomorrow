## Overview

Honolulu, USA — trade winds keep temperatures pleasant all year, making Honolulu one of the most consistently comfortable cities on Earth. Whether you are planning a holiday or a business trip, understanding the weather helps you pack right and make the most of your time.

## Temperature

Daytime highs reach **30°C (86°F)** while nights cool to **22°C (72°F)**. Expect around 9 hours of sunshine per day.

## Rainfall

Honolulu sees about **45mm of rainfall across 8 days** during this period. Rainfall is moderate. The odd shower is possible but unlikely to ruin your plans.

## Humidity and Comfort

Average humidity sits at **65%**. Most visitors find this comfortable, though midday can feel slightly muggy.

## What to Pack

- Lightweight breathable clothing in cotton or linen
- High-SPF sunscreen, sunglasses, and a wide-brimmed hat
- Compact umbrella or light rain jacket
- Comfortable walking shoes suited to the terrain

## Best Things to Do

- Sunbathe and surf at Waikiki Beach
- Hike Diamond Head crater for a panoramic sunrise view
- Snorkel at Hanauma Bay's protected reef
- Drive to the North Shore for big-wave watching in winter

## Travel Tips

- The windward (east) side gets more rain; Waikiki on the leeward side is drier
- Reef-safe sunscreen is required by Hawaii law
- Book snorkelling and hiking early; popular spots fill up fast
