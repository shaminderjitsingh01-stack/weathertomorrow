## Overview

Osaka, Japan — April is cherry blossom season, transforming Osaka into a pink-and-white wonderland — one of the most magical times to visit Japan. Whether you are planning a holiday or a business trip, understanding the weather helps you pack right and make the most of your time.

## Temperature

Daytime highs reach **20°C (68°F)** while nights cool to **10°C (50°F)**. Expect around 6 hours of sunshine per day.

## Rainfall

Osaka sees about **100mm of rainfall across 10 days** during this period. Showers are fairly regular — keep an umbrella handy when you head out.

## Humidity and Comfort

Average humidity sits at **60%**. Most visitors find this comfortable, though midday can feel slightly muggy.

## What to Pack

- Light layers — t-shirts plus a jacket for cooler evenings
- Sunscreen and sunglasses
- Compact umbrella or light rain jacket
- Comfortable walking shoes suited to the terrain

## Best Things to Do

- Picnic under cherry blossoms at Osaka Castle Park during hanami season
- Eat your way through Dotonbori's neon-lit street food strip
- Visit Osaka Castle and its museum for a panoramic city view
- Take a day trip to Nara to feed the friendly bowing deer

## Travel Tips

- Cherry blossom timing varies by year — check bloom forecasts before booking
- Get an IC transport card for seamless subway and train travel
- Osaka is Japan's food capital — budget generously for eating
