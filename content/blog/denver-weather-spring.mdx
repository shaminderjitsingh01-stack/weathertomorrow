## Overview

Denver, USA — spring in the Mile High City is unpredictable — sunshine one hour, snow the next — but the proximity to the Rockies and craft-beer culture more than compensate. Whether you are planning a holiday or a business trip, understanding the weather helps you pack right and make the most of your time.

## Temperature

Daytime highs reach **16°C (61°F)** while nights cool to **2°C (36°F)**. Expect around 8 hours of sunshine per day.

## Rainfall

Denver sees about **45mm of rainfall across 9 days** during this period. Rainfall is moderate. The odd shower is possible but unlikely to ruin your plans.

## Humidity and Comfort

Average humidity sits at **40%**. The air is pleasantly dry, making even warmer temperatures feel manageable.

## What to Pack

- Layered outfits — sweaters, a warm jacket, and a scarf
- Waterproof walking shoes
- Compact umbrella or light rain jacket
- Comfortable walking shoes suited to the terrain

## Best Things to Do

- Drive into Rocky Mountain National Park for elk sightings and alpine scenery
- Catch a concert or sunrise yoga at the Red Rocks Amphitheatre
- Explore the craft brewery scene on a Denver Beer Trail tour
- Walk the 16th Street Mall and visit Union Station's restaurants

## Travel Tips

- Spring snowstorms can hit as late as May — always check the forecast before mountain drives
- Altitude (1,600m) can cause headaches; drink extra water your first two days
- Sunshine is intense at altitude — wear sunscreen even on cool days
