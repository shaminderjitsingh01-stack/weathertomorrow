## Why October?

October is autumn magic — foliage in the Northern Hemisphere, spring in the south, and perfect conditions across the Mediterranean and Middle East.

## Top Destinations

### Kyoto, Japan

High: **22°C (72°F)** | Low: **12°C (54°F)**

The koyo (autumn foliage) season paints 2,000 temples in crimson and gold.

### New York, USA

High: **17°C (63°F)** | Low: **9°C (48°F)**

Central Park in autumn colours, crisp air, and the city's best walking weather.

### Petra, Jordan

High: **26°C (79°F)** | Low: **14°C (57°F)**

Warm, dry conditions perfect for exploring the ancient rose-red city.

### Oaxaca, Mexico

High: **25°C (77°F)** | Low: **12°C (54°F)**

Día de los Muertos preparations begin. Dry season, food culture, and mezcal country.

### Rwanda, Africa

High: **26°C (79°F)** | Low: **15°C (59°F)**

Short dry season — prime gorilla trekking conditions in Volcanoes National Park.

## How to Choose

| Priority | Best Fit |
|----------|----------|
| Beach and sun | Kyoto or New York |
| Culture and sightseeing | Petra or Oaxaca |
| Budget-friendly | Research shoulder-season pricing for each destination |
| Adventure | Rwanda |

## Packing Tips for October Travel

Shoulder season months mean variable weather. Pack layers and a rain jacket regardless of destination.
