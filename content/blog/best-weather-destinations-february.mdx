## Why February?

February is ideal for escaping winter grey. Carnival season lights up Brazil, the Canary Islands enjoy spring-like warmth, and Southeast Asia remains dry and sunny.

## Top Destinations

### Rio de Janeiro, Brazil

High: **32°C (90°F)** | Low: **24°C (75°F)**

Carnival energy, beach weather, and tropical heat. The city's biggest party and perfect beach days.

### Tenerife, Spain

High: **21°C (70°F)** | Low: **14°C (57°F)**

Europe's warmest winter destination — volcanic landscapes, beaches, and whale watching in mild sunshine.

### Hanoi, Vietnam

High: **20°C (68°F)** | Low: **14°C (57°F)**

Cool and dry — the most comfortable weather for exploring the Old Quarter and Ha Long Bay.

### Maldives, Indian Ocean

High: **31°C (88°F)** | Low: **26°C (79°F)**

Peak dry season with crystal-clear water, making it the best time for snorkelling and diving.

### Patagonia, Argentina/Chile

High: **18°C (64°F)** | Low: **6°C (43°F)**

Southern summer means long daylight for trekking Torres del Paine and Perito Moreno Glacier.

## How to Choose

| Priority | Best Fit |
|----------|----------|
| Beach and sun | Rio de Janeiro or Tenerife |
| Culture and sightseeing | Hanoi or Maldives |
| Budget-friendly | Research shoulder-season pricing for each destination |
| Adventure | Patagonia |

## Packing Tips for February Travel

Northern hemisphere destinations are in winter — pack warm layers. Southern hemisphere destinations are in summer — go light.
