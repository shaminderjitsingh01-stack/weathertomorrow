## Overview

Paris, France — autumn drapes Paris in golden leaves and soft light, making it arguably the most romantic season in the City of Light. Whether you are planning a holiday or a business trip, understanding the weather helps you pack right and make the most of your time.

## Temperature

Daytime highs reach **14°C (57°F)** while nights cool to **7°C (45°F)**. Expect around 4 hours of sunshine per day.

## Rainfall

Paris sees about **55mm of rainfall across 10 days** during this period. Rainfall is moderate. The odd shower is possible but unlikely to ruin your plans.

## Humidity and Comfort

Average humidity sits at **80%**. Combined with the heat this can feel oppressive. Stay hydrated, wear breathable fabrics, and take advantage of air-conditioned spaces during peak afternoon hours.

## What to Pack

- Layered outfits — sweaters, a warm jacket, and a scarf
- Waterproof walking shoes
- Compact umbrella or light rain jacket
- Comfortable walking shoes suited to the terrain

## Best Things to Do

- Visit the Louvre and Musée d'Orsay without summer crowds
- Walk along the Seine as chestnut trees turn gold
- Sip coffee at a pavement cafe in Saint-Germain-des-Prés
- Climb Montmartre for a misty view of the city's zinc rooftops

## Travel Tips

- Carry a compact umbrella — drizzle is common but rarely heavy
- This is fashion week and wine harvest season; restaurants can be busier than expected
- The Paris Museum Pass is worthwhile if you plan to visit three or more museums
