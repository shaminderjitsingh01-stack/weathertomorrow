## Overview

Marrakech, Morocco — spring is the ideal time to visit — warm without the extreme summer heat, wildflowers in the Atlas foothills, and the souks at their most pleasant. Whether you are planning a holiday or a business trip, understanding the weather helps you pack right and make the most of your time.

## Temperature

Daytime highs reach **26°C (79°F)** while nights cool to **12°C (54°F)**. Expect around 9 hours of sunshine per day.

## Rainfall

Marrakech sees about **25mm of rainfall across 4 days** during this period. Rain is uncommon, though a light sprinkle is not out of the question.

## Humidity and Comfort

Average humidity sits at **40%**. The air is pleasantly dry, making even warmer temperatures feel manageable.

## What to Pack

- Light layers — t-shirts plus a jacket for cooler evenings
- Sunscreen and sunglasses
- Comfortable walking shoes suited to the terrain

## Best Things to Do

- Lose yourself in the labyrinth of souks around Jemaa el-Fnaa square
- Visit the stunning Majorelle Garden created by Yves Saint Laurent
- Day-trip to the Atlas Mountains for Berber villages and valley hikes
- Watch the sunset storytellers and musicians fill Jemaa el-Fnaa

## Travel Tips

- Bargaining is expected in the souks — start at about a third of the asking price
- Dress modestly out of respect; shoulders and knees covered is appreciated
- Riads (traditional guesthouses) offer a far more authentic stay than hotels
