## Overview

San Diego, USA — San Diego boasts some of the most consistently pleasant weather in the US — warm, sunny, and mild virtually every day of the year. Whether you are planning a holiday or a business trip, understanding the weather helps you pack right and make the most of your time.

## Temperature

Daytime highs reach **22°C (72°F)** while nights cool to **14°C (57°F)**. Expect around 10 hours of sunshine per day.

## Rainfall

San Diego sees about **25mm of rainfall across 3 days** during this period. Rain is uncommon, though a light sprinkle is not out of the question.

## Humidity and Comfort

Average humidity sits at **65%**. Most visitors find this comfortable, though midday can feel slightly muggy.

## What to Pack

- Light layers — t-shirts plus a jacket for cooler evenings
- Sunscreen and sunglasses
- Comfortable walking shoes suited to the terrain

## Best Things to Do

- Spend a day at Balboa Park exploring museums and gardens
- Relax at La Jolla Cove and watch the sea lions bask on the rocks
- Visit the world-famous San Diego Zoo
- Stroll the Gaslamp Quarter for craft breweries and rooftop dining

## Travel Tips

- May Gray and June Gloom bring morning fog that clears by midday
- The San Diego Trolley connects the airport, downtown, and the border
- Craft beer is huge here — visit a few of the 150+ local breweries
