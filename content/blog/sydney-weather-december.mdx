## Overview

Sydney, Australia — December is early summer, beach season begins, and Sydney builds toward its spectacular New Year's Eve harbour fireworks. Whether you are planning a holiday or a business trip, understanding the weather helps you pack right and make the most of your time.

## Temperature

Daytime highs reach **26°C (79°F)** while nights cool to **18°C (64°F)**. Expect around 8 hours of sunshine per day.

## Rainfall

Sydney sees about **80mm of rainfall across 10 days** during this period. Rainfall is moderate. The odd shower is possible but unlikely to ruin your plans.

## Humidity and Comfort

Average humidity sits at **65%**. Most visitors find this comfortable, though midday can feel slightly muggy.

## What to Pack

- Light layers — t-shirts plus a jacket for cooler evenings
- Sunscreen and sunglasses
- Compact umbrella or light rain jacket
- Comfortable walking shoes suited to the terrain

## Best Things to Do

- Walk from Bondi to Coogee along the stunning coastal path
- See the Sydney Opera House and harbour on a ferry ride
- Take a day trip to the Blue Mountains for bush walks and lookouts
- Celebrate New Year's Eve with fireworks over the Harbour Bridge

## Travel Tips

- Apply sunscreen frequently — the UV index in Australia is extreme
- The Opal card works on all public transport including ferries
- NYE viewing spots fill up very early; arrive by midday for a good harbour position
