## Overview

Melbourne, Australia — January is peak summer, the Australian Open tennis fills the city with energy, and Melbourne's famous unpredictable weather keeps things exciting. Whether you are planning a holiday or a business trip, understanding the weather helps you pack right and make the most of your time.

## Temperature

Daytime highs reach **26°C (79°F)** while nights cool to **15°C (59°F)**. Expect around 9 hours of sunshine per day.

## Rainfall

Melbourne sees about **48mm of rainfall across 8 days** during this period. Rainfall is moderate. The odd shower is possible but unlikely to ruin your plans.

## Humidity and Comfort

Average humidity sits at **55%**. The air is pleasantly dry, making even warmer temperatures feel manageable.

## What to Pack

- Light layers — t-shirts plus a jacket for cooler evenings
- Sunscreen and sunglasses
- Compact umbrella or light rain jacket
- Comfortable walking shoes suited to the terrain

## Best Things to Do

- Watch the Australian Open live at Melbourne Park
- Explore the street art and coffee culture of the city's laneways
- Take the Great Ocean Road day trip to the Twelve Apostles
- Cool off at St Kilda Beach and walk the pier at sunset

## Travel Tips

- Melbourne can experience four seasons in one day — always carry a layer and an umbrella
- Slip, slop, slap: sunscreen, a hat, and shade are essential on hot days
- The free City Circle tram loops through the CBD's main attractions
