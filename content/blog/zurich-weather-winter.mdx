## Overview

Zurich, Switzerland — winter in Zurich means Christmas lights along Bahnhofstrasse, fondue by the lake, and world-class ski resorts just an hour away by train. Whether you are planning a holiday or a business trip, understanding the weather helps you pack right and make the most of your time.

## Temperature

Daytime highs reach **3°C (37°F)** while nights cool to **-2°C (28°F)**. Expect around 2 hours of sunshine per day.

## Rainfall

Zurich sees about **65mm of rainfall across 11 days** during this period. Rainfall is moderate. The odd shower is possible but unlikely to ruin your plans.

## Humidity and Comfort

Average humidity sits at **80%**. Combined with the heat this can feel oppressive. Stay hydrated, wear breathable fabrics, and take advantage of air-conditioned spaces during peak afternoon hours.

## What to Pack

- Insulated winter coat with thermal base layers
- Warm hat, gloves, and a scarf
- Insulated waterproof boots
- Compact umbrella or light rain jacket
- Comfortable walking shoes suited to the terrain

## Best Things to Do

- Browse Europe's largest indoor Christmas market at Zurich HB station
- Enjoy cheese fondue at a traditional restaurant in the Altstadt
- Walk the snow-dusted shores of Lake Zurich
- Take a day trip to Engelberg or Flumserberg for skiing or snowboarding

## Travel Tips

- Swiss trains are punctual and scenic — use them for ski day trips
- Winter clothing is expensive in Zurich; bring your own gear
- Hot chocolate at Sprüngli on Paradeplatz is a local institution
