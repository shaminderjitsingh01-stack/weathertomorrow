## How 25°C (77°F) Feels

Warm and pleasant — what most people consider ideal weather. Comfortable in short sleeves during the day, though evenings might call for a light layer.

## Recommended Layers

- T-shirt or light cotton shirt
- Shorts, a skirt, or light trousers
- Light dress for a versatile option
- Breathable sneakers or comfortable sandals

## Accessories

- Sunglasses
- Sunscreen SPF 30+
- A light cardigan for air-conditioned interiors

## What to Avoid

- Heavy fabrics like denim jackets
- Dark colours that absorb heat
- Tight-fitting synthetic fabrics that trap sweat

## Best Fabrics for 25°C

Linen, cotton, and moisture-wicking synthetics keep you cool. Avoid polyester that traps heat. Loose fits allow airflow around your body.

## Activity Considerations

Perfect for almost every outdoor activity. Running, cycling, hiking, and sightseeing are all comfortable. If you are sitting in direct sun for a long time, seek shade occasionally and stay hydrated.
