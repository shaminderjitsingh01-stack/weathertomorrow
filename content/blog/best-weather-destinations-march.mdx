## Why March?

March marks the transition from winter to spring in the Northern Hemisphere and autumn in the south. Shoulder season pricing meets increasingly pleasant weather.

## Top Destinations

### Marrakech, Morocco

High: **23°C (73°F)** | Low: **10°C (50°F)**

Warm but not hot — perfect for exploring the souks, gardens, and Atlas Mountains.

### Havana, Cuba

High: **28°C (82°F)** | Low: **19°C (66°F)**

Dry season with comfortable warmth. Classic cars, music, and colonial architecture in pleasant weather.

### Kyoto, Japan

High: **14°C (57°F)** | Low: **4°C (39°F)**

Early cherry blossoms begin in late March — temples framed by pink petals.

### Cancún, Mexico

High: **30°C (86°F)** | Low: **21°C (70°F)**

Dry, sunny, and warm before the summer humidity hits. Peak spring break destination.

### Queenstown, New Zealand

High: **18°C (64°F)** | Low: **5°C (41°F)**

Early autumn colours against mountain backdrops. Fewer tourists, great hiking weather.

## How to Choose

| Priority | Best Fit |
|----------|----------|
| Beach and sun | Marrakech or Havana |
| Culture and sightseeing | Kyoto or Cancún |
| Budget-friendly | Research shoulder-season pricing for each destination |
| Adventure | Queenstown |

## Packing Tips for March Travel

Shoulder season months mean variable weather. Pack layers and a rain jacket regardless of destination.
