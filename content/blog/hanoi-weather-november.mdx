## Overview

Hanoi, Vietnam — November marks the start of the cool, dry season — one of the most comfortable months to explore northern Vietnam. Whether you are planning a holiday or a business trip, understanding the weather helps you pack right and make the most of your time.

## Temperature

Daytime highs reach **25°C (77°F)** while nights cool to **17°C (63°F)**. Expect around 5 hours of sunshine per day.

## Rainfall

Hanoi sees about **40mm of rainfall across 6 days** during this period. Rainfall is moderate. The odd shower is possible but unlikely to ruin your plans.

## Humidity and Comfort

Average humidity sits at **75%**. Most visitors find this comfortable, though midday can feel slightly muggy.

## What to Pack

- Light layers — t-shirts plus a jacket for cooler evenings
- Sunscreen and sunglasses
- Compact umbrella or light rain jacket
- Comfortable walking shoes suited to the terrain

## Best Things to Do

- Wander the atmospheric Old Quarter streets on foot or by cyclo
- Enjoy a morning stroll around Hoan Kiem Lake and Ngoc Son Temple
- Take a street-food tour to try pho, bun cha, and egg coffee
- Book a two-day trip to Ha Long Bay while the weather is calm

## Travel Tips

- Carry a light jacket for cool evenings, especially near the lakes
- Traffic is chaotic — cross roads slowly and steadily without hesitating
- Vietnamese coffee is strong; try ca phe trung (egg coffee) at a lakeside cafe
