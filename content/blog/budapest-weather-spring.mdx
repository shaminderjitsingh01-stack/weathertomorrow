## Overview

Budapest, Hungary — spring warms the banks of the Danube, fills the ruin bars with optimism, and makes Budapest's thermal baths even more inviting. Whether you are planning a holiday or a business trip, understanding the weather helps you pack right and make the most of your time.

## Temperature

Daytime highs reach **17°C (63°F)** while nights cool to **7°C (45°F)**. Expect around 7 hours of sunshine per day.

## Rainfall

Budapest sees about **55mm of rainfall across 9 days** during this period. Rainfall is moderate. The odd shower is possible but unlikely to ruin your plans.

## Humidity and Comfort

Average humidity sits at **60%**. Most visitors find this comfortable, though midday can feel slightly muggy.

## What to Pack

- Layered outfits — sweaters, a warm jacket, and a scarf
- Waterproof walking shoes
- Compact umbrella or light rain jacket
- Comfortable walking shoes suited to the terrain

## Best Things to Do

- Soak in the Széchenyi or Gellért thermal baths on a cool morning
- Walk up to Buda Castle for sweeping views of the Parliament building
- Explore the unique ruin bars of the Jewish Quarter by night
- Take an evening Danube river cruise with Parliament lit up in gold

## Travel Tips

- Bring a swimsuit for the thermal baths — they are a must-do in any weather
- Spring can be changeable; a waterproof layer keeps you flexible
- The Budapest Card includes unlimited public transport and free museum entry
