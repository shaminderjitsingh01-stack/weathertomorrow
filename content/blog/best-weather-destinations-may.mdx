## Why May?

May is one of the best travel months — Europe warms up, monsoons have not yet hit Asia, and shoulder season pricing is still available in many destinations.

## Top Destinations

### Barcelona, Spain

High: **22°C (72°F)** | Low: **14°C (57°F)**

Warm Mediterranean sunshine without July's heat. Beach days and outdoor dining at their best.

### Santorini, Greece

High: **23°C (73°F)** | Low: **16°C (61°F)**

Picture-perfect weather before the summer crush. Blue domes and caldera views in comfortable warmth.

### Amalfi Coast, Italy

High: **22°C (72°F)** | Low: **14°C (57°F)**

Lemon groves in bloom, swimming season begins, and clifftop villages without August's crowds.

### Iceland, Nordic

High: **10°C (50°F)** | Low: **4°C (39°F)**

Days are getting long (19+ hours of light), puffins arrive, and the highlands begin opening.

### Peru, South America

High: **20°C (68°F)** | Low: **8°C (46°F)**

Dry season begins in the Andes. Clear skies for Machu Picchu and the Sacred Valley.

## How to Choose

| Priority | Best Fit |
|----------|----------|
| Beach and sun | Barcelona or Santorini |
| Culture and sightseeing | Amalfi Coast or Iceland |
| Budget-friendly | Research shoulder-season pricing for each destination |
| Adventure | Peru |

## Packing Tips for May Travel

Shoulder season months mean variable weather. Pack layers and a rain jacket regardless of destination.
