## Why August?

August is the hottest month in many Northern Hemisphere destinations. Smart travellers head north, to altitude, or to shoulder-season Southern Hemisphere spots.

## Top Destinations

### Norway Fjords, Norway

High: **18°C (64°F)** | Low: **10°C (50°F)**

Mild temperatures, long daylight, and dramatic fjord scenery at its most accessible.

### Swiss Alps, Switzerland

High: **20°C (68°F)** | Low: **10°C (50°F)**

Alpine meadows in full bloom, hiking at altitude where temperatures stay comfortable.

### Edinburgh, UK

High: **19°C (66°F)** | Low: **11°C (52°F)**

The Fringe Festival transforms the city into the world's largest arts venue.

### Montana, USA

High: **28°C (82°F)** | Low: **10°C (50°F)**

Glacier National Park, big skies, and dry warmth for hiking and fly fishing.

### Namibia, Africa

High: **25°C (77°F)** | Low: **7°C (45°F)**

Dry winter — best wildlife viewing at waterholes, plus surreal desert landscapes.

## How to Choose

| Priority | Best Fit |
|----------|----------|
| Beach and sun | Norway Fjords or Swiss Alps |
| Culture and sightseeing | Edinburgh or Montana |
| Budget-friendly | Research shoulder-season pricing for each destination |
| Adventure | Namibia |

## Packing Tips for August Travel

Northern hemisphere destinations are in summer — pack light, breathable clothing and sunscreen. Southern hemisphere destinations are in winter — bring layers.
