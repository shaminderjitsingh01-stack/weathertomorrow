## Why September?

September is the sweet spot — summer warmth lingers in the Mediterranean, crowds thin, and prices drop. One of the best months for travel.

## Top Destinations

### Tuscany, Italy

High: **26°C (79°F)** | Low: **14°C (57°F)**

Grape harvest, golden light, fewer tourists, and perfect temperatures for exploring hilltop towns.

### Santorini, Greece

High: **26°C (79°F)** | Low: **20°C (68°F)**

Warm sea temperatures, sunset caldera views, and summer crowds gone. Arguably the best month.

### Munich, Germany

High: **19°C (66°F)** | Low: **10°C (50°F)**

Oktoberfest begins in late September. Beer gardens, lederhosen, and Bavarian tradition.

### New England, USA

High: **21°C (70°F)** | Low: **10°C (50°F)**

Early fall foliage begins, apple orchards open, and coastal New England is at its prettiest.

### Patagonia, Chile/Argentina

High: **12°C (54°F)** | Low: **2°C (36°F)**

Spring arrives — wildflowers, newborn guanacos, and hiking trails reopening.

## How to Choose

| Priority | Best Fit |
|----------|----------|
| Beach and sun | Tuscany or Santorini |
| Culture and sightseeing | Munich or New England |
| Budget-friendly | Research shoulder-season pricing for each destination |
| Adventure | Patagonia |

## Packing Tips for September Travel

Shoulder season months mean variable weather. Pack layers and a rain jacket regardless of destination.
