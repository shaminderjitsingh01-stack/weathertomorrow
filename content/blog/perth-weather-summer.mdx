## Overview

Perth, Australia — summer in Perth is hot, dry, and gloriously sunny — perfect for beach days, wine regions, and the stunning Indian Ocean coastline. Whether you are planning a holiday or a business trip, understanding the weather helps you pack right and make the most of your time.

## Temperature

Daytime highs reach **31°C (88°F)** while nights cool to **18°C (64°F)**. Expect around 11 hours of sunshine per day.

## Rainfall

Perth sees about **10mm of rainfall across 2 days** during this period. Rain is uncommon, though a light sprinkle is not out of the question.

## Humidity and Comfort

Average humidity sits at **45%**. The air is pleasantly dry, making even warmer temperatures feel manageable.

## What to Pack

- Lightweight breathable clothing in cotton or linen
- High-SPF sunscreen, sunglasses, and a wide-brimmed hat
- Comfortable walking shoes suited to the terrain

## Best Things to Do

- Relax at Cottesloe Beach and watch the sunset over the Indian Ocean
- Ferry to Rottnest Island for snorkelling and quokka selfies
- Walk through Kings Park Botanic Garden for wildflowers and city views
- Drive to the Swan Valley for wine tastings and artisan food

## Travel Tips

- Apply sunscreen every two hours — the UV index is extreme in Australian summer
- The Fremantle Doctor sea breeze arrives most afternoons and drops temperatures quickly
- Perth is remote — plan driving distances carefully if heading out of the city
