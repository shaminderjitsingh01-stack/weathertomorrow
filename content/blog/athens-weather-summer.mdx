## Overview

Athens, Greece — summer brings scorching heat and blazing sunshine, but also long evenings, island-hopping, and the Acropolis bathed in golden light. Whether you are planning a holiday or a business trip, understanding the weather helps you pack right and make the most of your time.

## Temperature

Daytime highs reach **34°C (93°F)** while nights cool to **23°C (73°F)**. Expect around 12 hours of sunshine per day.

## Rainfall

Athens sees about **6mm of rainfall across 1 days** during this period. Rain is uncommon, though a light sprinkle is not out of the question.

## Humidity and Comfort

Average humidity sits at **40%**. The air is pleasantly dry, making even warmer temperatures feel manageable.

## What to Pack

- Lightweight breathable clothing in cotton or linen
- High-SPF sunscreen, sunglasses, and a wide-brimmed hat
- Comfortable walking shoes suited to the terrain

## Best Things to Do

- Visit the Acropolis at opening time (8am) before temperatures soar
- Take a ferry to Hydra, Aegina, or Poros for a day trip from Piraeus
- Dine at a rooftop taverna in Plaka with Parthenon views at sunset
- Explore the National Archaeological Museum in air-conditioned comfort

## Travel Tips

- The Acropolis has no shade — go early or late, never midday
- Tap water in Athens is safe and free; refill your bottle at restaurants
- Island ferries book up fast in July and August — reserve online
