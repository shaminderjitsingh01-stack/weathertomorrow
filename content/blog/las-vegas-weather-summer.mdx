## Overview

Las Vegas, USA — summer in the Mojave Desert means scorching heat, but pool parties, world-class shows, and air-conditioned casinos keep the fun going. Whether you are planning a holiday or a business trip, understanding the weather helps you pack right and make the most of your time.

## Temperature

Daytime highs reach **41°C (106°F)** while nights cool to **27°C (81°F)**. Expect around 13 hours of sunshine per day.

## Rainfall

Las Vegas sees about **10mm of rainfall across 2 days** during this period. Rain is uncommon, though a light sprinkle is not out of the question.

## Humidity and Comfort

Average humidity sits at **15%**. The air is pleasantly dry, making even warmer temperatures feel manageable.

## What to Pack

- Lightweight breathable clothing in cotton or linen
- High-SPF sunscreen, sunglasses, and a wide-brimmed hat
- Comfortable walking shoes suited to the terrain

## Best Things to Do

- Spend the afternoon at a resort pool party on the Strip
- Catch a Cirque du Soleil or headliner show in the evening
- Day-trip to the Grand Canyon or Red Rock Canyon early morning
- Explore the Fremont Street Experience after dark when it cools down

## Travel Tips

- Never underestimate the heat — heatstroke is a real risk above 40°C
- Drink water constantly, even if you do not feel thirsty
- The best hotel deals are midweek; weekends command premium prices
