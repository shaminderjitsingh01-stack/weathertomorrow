## Overview

Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia — the equatorial climate means heat and afternoon thunderstorms are a daily constant, but mornings are typically clear and bright. Whether you are planning a holiday or a business trip, understanding the weather helps you pack right and make the most of your time.

## Temperature

Daytime highs reach **33°C (91°F)** while nights cool to **24°C (75°F)**. Expect around 6 hours of sunshine per day.

## Rainfall

Kuala Lumpur sees about **250mm of rainfall across 17 days** during this period. Downpours can be heavy but usually pass quickly. Waterproof gear is essential.

## Humidity and Comfort

Average humidity sits at **80%**. Combined with the heat this can feel oppressive. Stay hydrated, wear breathable fabrics, and take advantage of air-conditioned spaces during peak afternoon hours.

## What to Pack

- Lightweight breathable clothing in cotton or linen
- High-SPF sunscreen, sunglasses, and a wide-brimmed hat
- Waterproof jacket and a sturdy umbrella
- Moisture-wicking fabrics and a refillable water bottle
- Comfortable walking shoes suited to the terrain

## Best Things to Do

- Visit the Petronas Twin Towers and KLCC Park early in the day
- Explore the Batu Caves and their rainbow staircase
- Eat your way through Jalan Alor night market
- Cool off in the air-conditioned splendour of Pavilion KL mall

## Travel Tips

- Plan outdoor activities for the morning; storms roll in most afternoons by 3 pm
- The Grab app is the easiest way to get around — taxis can be unpredictable
- Dress modestly when visiting mosques; free robes are usually available at the entrance
