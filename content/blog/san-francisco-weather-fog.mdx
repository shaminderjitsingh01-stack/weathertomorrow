## Overview

San Francisco, USA — summer fog is San Francisco's signature weather — Karl the Fog rolls through the Golden Gate most mornings before burning off by midday. Whether you are planning a holiday or a business trip, understanding the weather helps you pack right and make the most of your time.

## Temperature

Daytime highs reach **22°C (72°F)** while nights cool to **13°C (55°F)**. Expect around 9 hours of sunshine per day.

## Rainfall

San Francisco sees about **2mm of rainfall across 0 days** during this period. Rain is virtually nonexistent — expect clear, dry skies throughout.

## Humidity and Comfort

Average humidity sits at **75%**. Most visitors find this comfortable, though midday can feel slightly muggy.

## What to Pack

- Light layers — t-shirts plus a jacket for cooler evenings
- Sunscreen and sunglasses
- Comfortable walking shoes suited to the terrain

## Best Things to Do

- Watch the fog pour over the Golden Gate Bridge from Battery Spencer
- Explore Fisherman's Wharf and sample clam chowder in a sourdough bowl
- Ride a cable car from Union Square to Ghirardelli Square
- Ferry to Alcatraz Island — book weeks in advance

## Travel Tips

- Always bring layers; it can be 10 degrees colder near the ocean than inland
- Mark Twain never actually said 'the coldest winter I ever spent was a summer in San Francisco' but the sentiment is real
- Fog typically clears by noon — plan outdoor sightseeing for afternoon
