## Overview

Vancouver, Canada — fall means rain returns to the Pacific Northwest, but the city's mountains, forests, and cozy coffee culture shine through the grey skies. Whether you are planning a holiday or a business trip, understanding the weather helps you pack right and make the most of your time.

## Temperature

Daytime highs reach **10°C (50°F)** while nights cool to **4°C (39°F)**. Expect around 3 hours of sunshine per day.

## Rainfall

Vancouver sees about **180mm of rainfall across 18 days** during this period. Downpours can be heavy but usually pass quickly. Waterproof gear is essential.

## Humidity and Comfort

Average humidity sits at **85%**. Combined with the heat this can feel oppressive. Stay hydrated, wear breathable fabrics, and take advantage of air-conditioned spaces during peak afternoon hours.

## What to Pack

- Layered outfits — sweaters, a warm jacket, and a scarf
- Waterproof walking shoes
- Waterproof jacket and a sturdy umbrella
- Comfortable walking shoes suited to the terrain

## Best Things to Do

- Walk or bike the Stanley Park seawall with autumn colours as backdrop
- Browse the artisan stalls at Granville Island Public Market
- Cross the Capilano Suspension Bridge through misty old-growth forest
- Watch Pacific storms crash against the rocks at English Bay

## Travel Tips

- Invest in a high-quality waterproof jacket — umbrellas are no match for sideways rain
- Rain or shine, outdoor Vancouver is stunning — embrace it
- Coffee culture is serious here; explore independent roasters in Gastown and Main Street
