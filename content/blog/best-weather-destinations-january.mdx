## Why January?

January offers winter escapes to the tropics and southern hemisphere summer destinations. It is peak season for beach holidays and ski trips alike.

## Top Destinations

### Phuket, Thailand

High: **32°C (90°F)** | Low: **24°C (75°F)**

Dry season perfection — clear skies, calm seas, and warm waters ideal for diving and beach relaxation.

### Cape Town, South Africa

High: **28°C (82°F)** | Low: **16°C (61°F)**

Peak summer with long sunny days, perfect for Table Mountain, wine country, and beach hopping.

### Sydney, Australia

High: **27°C (81°F)** | Low: **19°C (66°F)**

Australian summer at its best — harbour cruises, Bondi Beach, and NYE afterglow.

### Dubai, UAE

High: **24°C (75°F)** | Low: **14°C (57°F)**

The most comfortable month — warm but not brutal. Ideal for outdoor sightseeing and desert tours.

### Costa Rica, Central America

High: **30°C (86°F)** | Low: **20°C (68°F)**

Dry season in the Pacific coast — rainforest, volcanoes, and beaches with minimal rain.

## How to Choose

| Priority | Best Fit |
|----------|----------|
| Beach and sun | Phuket or Cape Town |
| Culture and sightseeing | Sydney or Dubai |
| Budget-friendly | Research shoulder-season pricing for each destination |
| Adventure | Costa Rica |

## Packing Tips for January Travel

Northern hemisphere destinations are in winter — pack warm layers. Southern hemisphere destinations are in summer — go light.
