## Overview

Delhi, India — winter is the most pleasant season, with mild days perfect for exploring the city's Mughal and colonial heritage. Whether you are planning a holiday or a business trip, understanding the weather helps you pack right and make the most of your time.

## Temperature

Daytime highs reach **21°C (70°F)** while nights cool to **7°C (45°F)**. Expect around 8 hours of sunshine per day.

## Rainfall

Delhi sees about **15mm of rainfall across 2 days** during this period. Rain is uncommon, though a light sprinkle is not out of the question.

## Humidity and Comfort

Average humidity sits at **55%**. The air is pleasantly dry, making even warmer temperatures feel manageable.

## What to Pack

- Light layers — t-shirts plus a jacket for cooler evenings
- Sunscreen and sunglasses
- Comfortable walking shoes suited to the terrain

## Best Things to Do

- Explore the Red Fort and Jama Masjid in the crisp morning air
- Visit Qutub Minar and Humayun's Tomb without summer's punishing heat
- Take a food walk through Old Delhi's legendary street-food lanes
- Day-trip to Agra for the Taj Mahal in soft winter light

## Travel Tips

- Fog can cause flight delays in December and January — build in buffer time
- Air quality dips in winter; check the AQI and consider a mask
- Mornings and evenings are chilly — a warm jacket is needed after dark
